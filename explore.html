San Luis Obispo

Hiking

There are lots of outdoor activities to do during quarentine in San Luis Obispo. Going on a hike is a great way to get out of your house while maintaining soical distancing. Some recommended hikes to go on are East Cuesta Ridge, Madonna Mountain Loop, and Reservoir Canyon Trail.

Downtown San Luis Obispo

With business gradually opening, downtown is coming alive again. Stop by one of the many restaurants downtown, grab some food to go, and have a picnic at the mission. Walk the blocks of downtown and take in the architecture of these beautiful buildings. Stop by McConnell's Ice cream and grab a pint to go!
